Port Blair, Havelock

Top things to do in Port Blair

Port Blair, the capital of the Andaman Islands, is a hub of activities ranging from historical explorations to marine adventures. Key attractions include the historical Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Island with its ruins and light and sound show, North Bay Island known for its water sports and coral safaris, and the serene Corbyn's Cove Beach for relaxation. Chidiyatapu offers bird watching and sunsets, while Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park showcases rich marine biodiversity. Evening entertainment includes the Cellular Jail Light and Sound Show and dinner cruises. Adventurous activities like scuba diving, snorkeling, and trekking cater to thrill-seekers, making Port Blair a diverse and captivating destination.

Top things to do in Havelock

Havelock Island is an adventurer's paradise, offering a rich blend of activities for its visitors. Key highlights include indulging in a variety of water sports like snorkeling, sea walking, and parasailing for underwater exploration, and kayaking or glass-bottom boat rides for those preferring to stay dry. Trek to the secluded Elephant Beach through dense forests for a touch of adventure and water sports. Savor the local cuisine, especially the seafood at numerous island restaurants. Capture the island's picturesque landscapes and vibrant marine life through photography. Experience the tranquil sunsets at Radhanagar Beach and stunning sunrises at Kalapathar Beach. Havelock Island encapsulates the essence of nature, adventure, and relaxation, making it a must-visit destination.

CARBYN’S COVE BEACH- LIGHT AND SOUND SHOW

After you land at Port Blaire Airport, we will escort you to the hotel. Get refreshed and have your lunch, and we will move to the first destination of the trip, Carbyn’s Cove Beach, where the breathtaking beauty of nature has ornamented the beach with the soothing sight of coconut and palm-fringed picturesque. Blend with the romantic nature of the coastline where you will enjoy the glittering smooth white sand and the blue water beside it. Check out the Light and Sound show at the evening at the Cellular jail, where you will get to know the detail of the freedom struggle through light and sound effects.

DAY 2: HAVELOCK ISLAND-RADHANAGAR BEACH

We will start the second day with an adventurous boat ride trip to the beautiful Havelock island, where people come to enjoy in the living Paradise. It is the place which has been uniquely carved by nature to present the true essence of beauty. Surrounded by the lush of greenery, the island is the best place for you to spend your alone time. From the Havelock island, we will go to Radhanagar Beach, which has been considered as Aasia’s 7th largest sea beach by the Times magazine. Let the turquoise blue sea mesmerise you with the enchanting thrill and blend in the moment of mysticism. The day will be called with an overnight stay at the Havelock island.

PORT BLAIR CITY TOUR

After having breakfast, we will go to the central city of Port Blair on the third day morning. Later, in the afternoon, after reaching Port Blair, the tour will start with the different attractions in the city, like the Fisheries Museum, where we will visit the exhibit of marine species of the island. The Naval Marine user, where we will get to know a lot about the history, geography and the archaeology of the islands along with the marine life, shells and corals. We will also get to the Anthropological Museum where the tribal essentials, their history and the artefacts are laid. Chatham Saw Mill will be another destination for us to visit, which is considered as one of Asia’s oldest and biggest sawmills. And at last, but not the least at all, Cellular jail, famous as the Kalapani, will be our visiting destination, where we will be revisiting the history.

EXCURSION TO ROSS ISLAND- CHIDIYATAPU

Feel the excitement in the enthralling Ross Island, which has been the ancient British Capital, on the fifth day of the trip. Get to visit the ruins of the establishment of the British buildings. You will find the old Church, Cemetery, official buildings, and more and go back into the glorious past. In the evening we will be heading to the bird’s place, Chidiyatapu. Visit the site at the time of sunset, and you will get blended with the beautiful scenic beauty of the island. Relish the sunset hue and bind yourselves to the enchanting essence of the moment.

DROP AT AIRPORT

We will drop you at the Airport, so you can get home back safe and secured with the everlasting memories of your wonderful vacation.
