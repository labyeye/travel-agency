Port Blair, Havelock

Top things to do in Port Blair

Port Blair, the capital of the Andaman Islands, is a hub of activities ranging from historical explorations to marine adventures. Key attractions include the historical Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Island with its ruins and light and sound show, North Bay Island known for its water sports and coral safaris, and the serene Corbyn's Cove Beach for relaxation. Chidiyatapu offers bird watching and sunsets, while Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park showcases rich marine biodiversity. Evening entertainment includes the Cellular Jail Light and Sound Show and dinner cruises. Adventurous activities like scuba diving, snorkeling, and trekking cater to thrill-seekers, making Port Blair a diverse and captivating destination.

Top things to do in Havelock

Havelock Island is an adventurer's paradise, offering a rich blend of activities for its visitors. Key highlights include indulging in a variety of water sports like snorkeling, sea walking, and parasailing for underwater exploration, and kayaking or glass-bottom boat rides for those preferring to stay dry. Trek to the secluded Elephant Beach through dense forests for a touch of adventure and water sports. Savor the local cuisine, especially the seafood at numerous island restaurants. Capture the island's picturesque landscapes and vibrant marine life through photography. Experience the tranquil sunsets at Radhanagar Beach and stunning sunrises at Kalapathar Beach. Havelock Island encapsulates the essence of nature, adventure, and relaxation, making it a must-visit destination.

AIRPORT PICKUP LATER VISIT CELLULAR JAIL AND CHIDIATAPU SUNSET TRIP + COMPLIMENTARY HONEYMOON CAKE

Arrive at Port Blair in the morning/afternoon by flight and our representative will be waiting outside holding a placard with your name on it. You will then be taken to your hotel where you rest and relax.

Later in the afternoon, we will first visit the National memorial. Cellular Jail is known for its unique jail architecture where Indian freedom fighters faced a lot of punishments from the Britishers. The struggle for Indian freedom and life of Indian freedom fighters can be learned when at this historic place.

Later we being our journey to the best sunset point in the Andaman archipelago, Chidiatapu. Explore the beauty of this amazing destination on the first day of your trip in the Andaman Islands. Chidiatapu offers one of the best sunset experiences in Andaman. Post this trip we return and drop you back at your hotel.

A Complimentary honeymoon cake is provided to our guests at Port Blair on the day of your arrival to Port Blair.

PORT BLAIR TO HAVELOCK IN CRUISE. LATER RADHANAGAR BEACH SUNSET TRIP + COMPLIMENTARY PHOTOSHOOT AND BEACH SIDE CANDLE LIT DINNER

Today, we depart to the Andaman Islands' most amazing destination, Havelock Island. This destination is rated as the best island in India. Depart from Port Blair to Havelock Island on a ferry. Upon reaching Havelock, our representative will attend to you and will drop you at your hotel. Rest and Relax in the beachside resort. Later, we begin our journey to one of Asia's best beaches, Radhanagar Beach. Explore this white sand beach and bask in the beauty of this natural wonder. Post this we drop you back at your resort where you relax and unwind.

Note: Candle Lit Dinner by the beach will be arranged on the day of your trip. A perfect setup for honeymoon couples in Andaman.

ELEPHANT BEACH BOAT RIDE TOUR AND COMPLIMENTARY SNORKELLING WITH HOTEL PICKUP AND DROP

Today, we begin our journey to the most visited snorkeling destination in Havelock Island, Elephant Beach. Around 30 Minutes by boat, Elephant Beach flaunts one of the best reefs on Havelock Island.

The great thing is that the reef here starts at a depth of less than a meter. Upon reaching Elephant beach, we rest and relax.

Later, a guide takes you to provide a complimentary snorkeling session at Elephant Beach (Snorkeling at Havelock Island depends upon the association guidelines). After the trip, we return back from Elephant beach to the Havelock dock in a boat. At the dock, our team attends you and drops you at your hotel.

KALAPATHAR BEACH + HAVELOCK TO PORT BLAIR IN CRUISE AND SHOPPING TRIP

Visit the amazing Kalapathar beach on Havelock Island. Click some amazing snaps that you will love for sure. Post this trip we take a cruise back to Port Blair from Havelock Island. Enjoy the beautiful cruise ride at the Andaman sea. After you reach back to Port Blair, our team will attend you and take you for a shopping experience. Explore local shopping areas on the islands and get a beautiful experience. Purchase gifts and souvenirs for your family. Post this trip we drop you back at your hotel for an overnight stay.

DROP TO THE AIRPORT

Check out from the hotel and proceed to the airport to return home with sweet memories of this exotic destination. Do note that hotels have an early check-out time. Guest will need to keep their luggage in the reception kiosk if your scheduled flight is in the afternoon. If you wish to visit any attraction during your free time then do let us know.
