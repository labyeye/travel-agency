Port Blair, Havelock, Neil

Top things to do in Port Blair

Port Blair, the capital of the Andaman Islands, is a hub of activities ranging from historical explorations to marine adventures. Key attractions include the historical Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose Island with its ruins and light and sound show, North Bay Island known for its water sports and coral safaris, and the serene Corbyn's Cove Beach for relaxation. Chidiyatapu offers bird watching and sunsets, while Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park showcases rich marine biodiversity. Evening entertainment includes the Cellular Jail Light and Sound Show and dinner cruises. Adventurous activities like scuba diving, snorkeling, and trekking cater to thrill-seekers, making Port Blair a diverse and captivating destination.

Top things to do in Havelock

Havelock Island is an adventurer's paradise, offering a rich blend of activities for its visitors. Key highlights include indulging in a variety of water sports like snorkeling, sea walking, and parasailing for underwater exploration, and kayaking or glass-bottom boat rides for those preferring to stay dry. Trek to the secluded Elephant Beach through dense forests for a touch of adventure and water sports. Savor the local cuisine, especially the seafood at numerous island restaurants. Capture the island's picturesque landscapes and vibrant marine life through photography. Experience the tranquil sunsets at Radhanagar Beach and stunning sunrises at Kalapathar Beach. Havelock Island encapsulates the essence of nature, adventure, and relaxation, making it a must-visit destination.

Top things to do in Neil

Neil Island, also known as Shaheed Dweep, is celebrated for its vibrant marine life and organic farming practices. A must-do is scuba diving and glass-bottom boat riding at Bharatpur Beach, offering an intimate glimpse into the underwater world distinct from other islands. Laxmanpur Beach is famed for its stunning sunsets, providing a peaceful end to your day with no water activities to disturb the tranquility. Adventurous souls can trek to the Natural Rock Formation, known locally as Howrah Bridge, revealing nature’s artistry at low tide. The island encapsulates serene beauty, making it a pristine spot for both relaxation and exploration, distinguishing itself with a laid-back vibe and less crowded spaces, inviting travelers to delve into its unexplored marine biodiversity.

AIRPORT PICKUP. LATER CARBYNS COVE BEACH AND COMPLIMENTARY JET SKI RIDE. LATER VISIT TO CELLULAR JAIL & LIGHT AND SOUND SHOW

Arrive at Port Blair in the morning/afternoon by flight and our representative will be waiting outside the airport holding a placard with your name on it.
You will be taken to your hotel where you rest and relax. Later we proceed to Corbyn's Cove.

Coastal drive to this beach is awesome, a stretch of around 4 Km with an open sea towards your left is a sight to experience. 

Take pit stops during this ride to click pictures and explore the beauty. At Corbyns Cove beach indulge in a complimentary Jet Ski ride and start your Andaman Island experience.

Later proceed to attend the enthralling Sound and Light Show at Cellular Jail - where the heroic saga of the Indian freedom struggle is brought alive. A light and sound show gets conducted inside the Cellular Jail explaining the rich history and struggles of the freedom fighters in the jail.

Post completion of this trip, we attend and drop you back at your hotel. show. Blend in the atmosphere and relish the ever-memorable experience.

BARATANG ISLAND : LIMESTONE CAVE & MUD VOLCANO

Depart to Baratang Island. by road through the dense tropical forest and Jarawa Tribal reserve in a convoy. Often you might see a member of the Jarawa Tribe while passing through the tribal reserve. At the end of this forest journey you would reach Baratang jetty. Launch service is available to cross the sea bay. Enjoy the breathtaking fiber boat ride through a fascinating Mangrove drive, which leads to the start point of the trek to Limestone caves. Explore this white sand beach and bask in the beauty of this natural wonder. Post this we drop you back at your resort where you relax and unwind.

Trek through dense forest to reach the Limestone Cave Made up of the mineral calcite (calcium carbonate) the cave contains beautiful cave formation. Return back for overnight stay at respective hotel at Port Blair by the same route.

Note: A Personal AC vehicle will be provided for your trip to Baratang Island.

PORT BLAIR TO HAVELOCK IN CRUISE. LATER RADHANAGAR BEACH VISIT UNTIL SUNSET + COMPLIMENTARY BEACH PHOTOSHOOT

Today we depart to the Andaman Islands most amazing destination,  Havelock Island This destination is rated as the best island in India. Depart fromPort Blair to Havelock Island on a ferry.Upon reaching Havelock, our representative attends you and will drop you at your hotel. Later we begin our journey to one of Asia's best beaches, Radhanagar Beach.

Explore this white sand beach and bask in the beauty of this natural wonder. You stay at this beach until sunset. Enjoy this Blue Flag-certified beach.

A professional photoshoot is arranged to capture the special memory that will hold a special place down memory lane. We recommend travelers a photoshoot experience by the beach. It's amazing when you are at Radhanagar Beach.

Post this we drop you back to your resort where you relax and unwind.

ELEPHANT BEACH BOAT RIDE TOUR AND COMPLIMENTARY SNORKELLING WITH HOTEL PICKUP AND DROP

Well, how about exploring something underwater? Today, we begin our journey to the most visited snorkeling destination in Havelock Island, Elephant Beach. Around 30 Minutes by boat,  Elephant Beach  flaunts one of the best reefs on  Havelock Island..

The great thing is that the reef here starts at a depth of less than a meter. Upon reaching Elephant beach, we rest and relax.  Later, a guide takes you to provide a complimentary snorkeling session at Elephant Beach (Snorkeling at Havelock Island depends upon the association guidelines). After the trip, we return back from Elephant beach to the Havelock dock in a boat. At the dock, our team attends you and drops you at your hotel.

Note: Our team at Havelock will guide you in case you require any assistance on the ground.

HAVELOCK TO NEIL ISLAND IN CRUISE. LATER VISIT LAXMANPUR BEACH FOR SUNSET

Check out from the hotel in Havelock Island and board a premium cruise to Neil Island, upon reaching the island our representative will take you to respective will assist you to your resort to check-in. Rest at the hotel and then proceed to visit Laxmanpur Beach This is a secluded beach that lies 2 km north of Neil's jetty. Experience a magnificent sunset at this beach. Post this we drop you back to your hotel in Neil Island.

Ferry Timing from Havelock to Neil Island is around 10:00 AM. Ferry timings are subject to change and team will update you in case of such changes.

After breakfast, we begin our journey to visit Natural rock formation . which is a major attraction at Neil Island.  Post this we visit  Bharatpur Beach. and explore the beauty at at Neil.
